# Code of conduct

DecisionAgentBench is an evidence-oriented, respectful research community. Participants must be
welcoming across identity, background, experience, and viewpoint; critique claims and artifacts,
not people; protect private information; and disclose conflicts that affect research review.

Harassment, discriminatory conduct, threats, doxxing, sexualized attention, deliberate disclosure
of secrets or personal data, and sustained bad-faith disruption are unacceptable in project spaces
or when representing the project.

Report conduct concerns privately through the repository's GitHub security-advisory channel until
a dedicated contact is published. Maintainers will acknowledge reports, minimize unnecessary
disclosure, hear relevant parties where safe, and take proportionate action including correction,
warning, temporary restriction, or removal. Retaliation against a good-faith reporter is itself a
violation.

Maintainers must recuse themselves when they are involved in a report. This code applies to issues,
pull requests, discussions, reviews, events, and project-affiliated communication.
